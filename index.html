Women's Safety Laws

Understanding your legal rights is crucial for protection and empowerment. Here are key laws that protect women in India.

The Protection of Women from Domestic Violence Act, 2005

This Act provides protection to women from domestic violence. It covers physical, emotional, sexual, and economic abuse.

Key Provisions: Protection orders, residence orders, monetary relief, and custody orders.

The Sexual Harassment of Women at Workplace Act, 2013

This law aims to protect women from sexual harassment at their workplace.

Key Provisions: Mandatory Internal Complaints Committee, local complaints committee, and protection against false complaints.

The Criminal Law (Amendment) Act, 2013

Also known as the Nirbhaya Act, this law amended the Indian Penal Code after the 2012 Delhi gang rape case.

Key Provisions: Stricter punishments for sexual offenses, new offenses like acid attack and stalking.

The Prohibition of Child Marriage Act, 2006

This law prohibits the marriage of children (below 18 for girls, 21 for boys) in India.

Key Provisions: Child marriages are voidable, punishment for conducting child marriage.

The Dowry Prohibition Act, 1961

This law prohibits the giving or taking of dowry in India.

Key Provisions: Penalty for giving or taking dowry, penalty for demanding dowry.

The Equal Remuneration Act, 1976

This law provides for payment of equal remuneration to men and women workers for same work or work of similar nature.

Key Provisions: Prevention of discrimination in recruitment and service conditions.

About Safe Haven

Learn more about our mission, vision, and the team behind Safe Haven.

Our Mission

To create a world where every woman feels safe, empowered, and supported through accessible resources, education, and community.

Our Vision

A society free from gender-based violence where women can live, work, and thrive without fear.

Our Values

Empathy, empowerment, inclusivity, and action-driven solutions for women's safety and well-being.

Our Impact

Our Story

Safe Haven was founded in 2020 by a group of women who experienced firsthand the challenges of navigating safety concerns in daily life. What started as a small community initiative has grown into a nationwide platform serving thousands of women each month.

We partner with local authorities, NGOs, and community organizations to provide comprehensive safety solutions tailored to the unique needs of women across different regions and circumstances.

Our Team

Our diverse team includes safety experts, counselors, legal advisors, and technology specialists all dedicated to our mission.

Partnerships

We collaborate with over 50 organizations nationwide to extend our reach and impact.
